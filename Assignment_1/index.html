About EventHub

EventHub is designed for the university community. It gives students, professors, and staff a simple way to create events, discover new opportunities, register to attend, and share feedback afterward.

The platform helps organizers manage capacity, waiting lists, and participant engagement, while making campus life more connected and accessible for everyone.

Main platform goals

  • Centralize university events in one platform
  • Make event registration easy and transparent
  • Support organizers with event management tools
  • Help participants choose events through reviews and ratings
